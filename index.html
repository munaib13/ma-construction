About Us

A local contractor, built on trust

MA Construction Bomai Zaingair is a builders & contracting company serving Sopore and the surrounding areas of Kashmir. We take on residential and commercial construction from the first foundation trench to the final coat of paint, keeping every client informed at every stage of the build.

  • Direct communication with the contractor — no layers, no confusion.
  • Skilled mason, plumbing, electrical and finishing teams working under one roof.
  • Material quality and site progress you can inspect at any time.
  • Work planned around your budget, your timeline and your land.
